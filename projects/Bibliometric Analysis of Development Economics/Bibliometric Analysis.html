Bibliometric Analysis of Development Economics (in progress).

May 2024 - Present

I worked as a part-time research assistant for professor Guojun He in Economics at the University of Hong Kong, and the work is supervised by postdoc Yuchen Quan at the University of Hong Kong.

My work includes data collection and analysis, specifically using Python to crawl literature titles, publication dates, journal repositories, abstracts, and PDFs from CNKI, and further convert the PDFs to text format for textual analysis. The work primarily involves writing Python scripts to crawl data and applying machine learning techniques.
