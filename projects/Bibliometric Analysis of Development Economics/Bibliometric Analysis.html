Bibliometric Analysis of Development Economics (in progress).

May 2024 - Present

I am working as a part-time research assistant for professor Guojun He in Economics at the University of Hong Kong, and the work is supervised by postdoc Yuchen Quan at the University of Hong Kong.

I leveraged Python-based browser automation, network request monitoring, HTML parsing, and multithreading techniques to scrape the names and personal information of 69,665 environmental engineers from the Environmental Registration Credit Platform. I then utilized the list I obtained to scrape 19,329,964 corresponding literature records from CNKI's dissertation database, improving the scraping speed by over five times through Python automation and asynchronous programming. Subsequently, I undertook the substantial task of cleaning a large dataset, integrating the author and literature datasets. By analyzing the professional qualifications, job requirements, and graduation timelines of environmental assessment engineers, I inferred the related literature and graduation time of all authors.
