China's Monetary Policy (in progress).

Jul 2024 - Present

I am working as a part-time research assistant for professor Kaiji Chen at the Emory University.

We hope to build a Chinese version of Taylor Rule. My work includes the construction of China's macroeconomic data set, as well as the creation and testing of the time-switching interest rate rule. The main programming language used is Matlab.
