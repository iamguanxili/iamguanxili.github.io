Teaching Assistant of Econometrics (Undergraduate Level).

Jan 2024 - Jun 2024

I worked as an teaching assistant of econometrics for professor Fei Jin at Fudan University in spring 2024.

I graded and assigned weekly problem sets on econometrics exercises and mid-term presentations with a class size of 60 students. I communicated with students about math solving problems, research intuitions and logistical issues during office hours. I also engaged students in relevant activities, such as regression methods, quasi-experiments, non-parametric methods, and use of statistical software, including Stata and Python.
