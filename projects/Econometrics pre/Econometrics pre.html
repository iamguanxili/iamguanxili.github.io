Presentation on Political Hierarchy and Regional Economic Development.

Apr 2023 - May 2023

This is a presentation by me on Political Hierarchy and Regional Economic Development: Evidence from a Spatial Discontinuity in China, written by Junxue Jia et al., published in Journal of Public Economics 194(2021). This is an independent presentation, designed to introduce cutting-edge research and guide students in a more meticulous study of Regression Discontinuity Design.

I thoroughly read the article, replicated the core results using the replication package, correst a slight mistake in the paper and made a presentation.
