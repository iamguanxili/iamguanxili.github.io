Estimating the Free Trade Zone Policy.

Jun 2023 - Sep 2023

I worked as a research assistant for this research, conducted by professor Suhua Tian at Fudan University. I collected the policy documents related to China's negative lists for free trade on the official website from 2015 to 2023. Then I digitized the policy regulations and analyzed the annual changes in the list's regulations.
