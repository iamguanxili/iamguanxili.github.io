The Impact of Trade with China on US Labor Market.

Mar 2024 - Apr 2024

This is an group research on the impact of trade with China on US Labor Market, with Jingxiang Huang at Fudan University.

We conducted a detailed literature review on the impact of US-China trade on US labor market, particularly Autor, Dorn and Hansen (2013) and Calienda, Dvorkin and Parro (2019).

We delivered a forty-five-minute presentation. I am responsible for all the empirical parts.
