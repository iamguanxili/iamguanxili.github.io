A Sufficient Statics Approach for Macro Policy (in progress).

This is an independent research by me of using a sufficient statics approach to evaluate maro policy, supervised by associate professor Wenbin Wu at Fudan University. This work is based on the sufficient statistics model developed by RÃ©gis Barnichon and Geert Mesters (2023).

I replicated the main results of the paper using Bayesian Vector Autoregression and sufficient statistics.

Subsequently, I gathered the latest data and employed this method to compute the optimal monetary policy of the Federal Reserve from 2020 to 2024. I examined the optimality of federal funds rate policy in 2021 and 2023, as well as the yield policy in 2020 and 2022. Furthermore, I assessed whether the Federal Reserve's policies over the past five years were optimal given conditional constraints.

I intend to utilize this method to further investigate the optimality of China's "Four Trillion Yuan Plan" implemented by the central bank in 2008.

The paper is in preparation.
